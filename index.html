Welcome! I'm Prof Herd, and I'm glad you stopped by.

Background

At 29, I'm a proud husband and father of four amazing kids, and my life is filled with adventure, love, and a lot of laughter. When I'm not spending quality time with my family, you'll probably find me indulging in my passions, like going on long walks, cooking up new recipes, or restoring classic cars. There's something incredibly satisfying about bringing old cars back to life, and it's a hobby that keeps me grounded and connected to a sense of craftsmanship.

Family and Balance

As a dedicated parent and partner, I understand the value of hard work, but also the importance of taking time for the things that make life meaningful. I believe in creating lasting memories with my loved ones, whether that's through simple moments like cooking dinner together or exploring new places. I'm always striving to find that perfect balance between family, hobbies, and personal growth, and that's something I'm passionate about sharing with others.

Learning and Growing

Whether I'm tinkering with cars or perfecting a new recipe, I'm constantly evolving. I love diving into new challenges and improving my skills, and I'm always looking for ways to expand my horizons. This mindset keeps me curious and driven, and I'm excited about what lies ahead as I continue to explore my passions and embrace the journey of life.
